Principal's Message

Vivekanand Pratishthan is a sacred center in the field of education. We extend a heartfelt welcome to all parents. The foundation of a child's physical, mental, and social development is laid during early childhood, from birth to six years of age. During this period, 70% of mental development and 40% of physical development takes place. How a child will lead life in the future largely depends on the family environment.

In short, if the foundation of a child's development during early childhood is strong, a balanced personality will be formed. Hence, do not rush to make children grow up by taking away their childhood. Provide nutritious food, love, care, protection, quality time, and have faith in them — everything will fall into place naturally.
