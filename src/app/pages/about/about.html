Vivekanand Pratishthan's
Dr Avinash Acharya Vidyalay, Jalgaon

Vivekanand Pratishthan's Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyayal stands as a beacon of educational excellence, catering to students from pre-primary to primary levels. Established under the esteemed banner of Vivekanand Pratishthan, the institution is driven by a steadfast commitment to providing top-notch education that transcends mere academics. Here, the emphasis lies not only on intellectual growth but also on the holistic development of each child, nurturing their minds, bodies, and spirits.

At the core of Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyayal's educational philosophy is a dynamic curriculum meticulously crafted to stimulate creativity, foster critical thinking, and instill moral values in students. This curriculum serves as a guiding light, empowering young learners with the skills and knowledge essential for navigating the complexities of the modern world with confidence and resilience. Through a blend of innovative teaching methodologies and a profound reverence for India's rich cultural heritage, the school endeavors to cultivate well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also compassionate and socially responsible citizens.

Driven by the belief that education is the cornerstone of societal progress, Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyayal is dedicated to nurturing a generation of leaders who will contribute positively to the betterment of society. By providing a conducive learning environment, encouraging active parental involvement, and fostering a deep sense of social responsibility, the school aims to empower its students to become catalysts for positive change in their communities and beyond. In essence, Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyayal embodies the vision of Vivekanand Pratishthan, striving to mold compassionate, responsible, and globally aware individuals who will shape a brighter future for generations to come.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

-Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya

Principal's Message

          Vivekanand Pratishthan is a sacred center in the field of education. We extend a heartfelt welcome to all parents. The foundation of a child's physical, mental, and social development is laid during early childhood, that is, from birth to six years of age. During this period, 70% of mental development and 40% of physical development takes place. How a child will lead life in the future largely depends on the family environment. Therefore the atmosphere at home should be supportive and nurturing for a child's proper development.
                    In short, if the foundation of a child's development during early childhood is strong, a balanced personality will be formed. Hence, do not rush to make children grow up by taking away their childhood. Avoid saying that you do not have time for your own child. Instead, provide your child with nutritious food, love, care, protection and quality time, and have faith in them - everything will fall into place naturally.
