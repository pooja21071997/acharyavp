Interactive Class

The interactive classroom is a hub of engaging and collaborative learning experiences. Through interactive whiteboards, multimedia presentations, and educational software, students participate in dynamic lessons that bring subjects to life.

They actively engage with the curriculum, answering questions, solving problems, and exploring concepts through hands-on activities. The interactive nature of the classroom fosters a sense of curiosity and enthusiasm among students, encouraging them to ask questions, share ideas, and collaborate with their peers.

Whether they’re embarking on virtual field trips, participating in interactive quizzes, or conducting science experiments, the interactive classroom provides a stimulating environment where every student can actively participate in their own learning journey.

Music

In the vibrant world of music at Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyalay’s primary section, students embark on a melodic journey exploring rhythm, melody, and harmony.

Through engaging lessons and hands-on experiences, they discover the joy of playing various musical instruments, harmonizing in choirs, and expressing themselves through song.

From learning traditional Indian melodies to experimenting with global rhythms, music becomes a vehicle for self-expression, creativity, and cultural appreciation, enriching their lives with the universal language of melody.

Drawing

In the colorful realm of drawing, young artists at Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyalay’s primary section unleash their imagination and creativity on canvas.

Guided by skilled instructors, they explore a variety of artistic techniques, from sketching and shading to painting and mixed media.

With each stroke of the brush and each line drawn, they learn to communicate their thoughts, emotions, and observations visually, cultivating a deep appreciation for the beauty and power of art as a means of expression and communication.

Dance

In the dynamic world of dance, students at Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyalay’s primary section embark on a journey of movement, rhythm, and expression.

Through a diverse range of dance styles, from classical Indian forms to contemporary genres, they learn the art of storytelling through motion, mastering intricate choreography and developing poise, grace, and stage presence.

As they sway to the rhythm of the music, dance becomes a celebration of culture, a form of physical fitness, and a source of joy and self-confidence, enriching their lives with the transformative power of movement.

Art & Craft

In the imaginative world of art and craft, young creators at Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyalay’s primary section explore a myriad of materials and techniques to bring their artistic visions to life.

From sculpting and pottery to paper crafting and textile art, they unleash their creativity, honing their fine motor skills and cultivating a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and aesthetics.

Through hands-on projects and guided experimentation, they learn to think critically, solve problems creatively, and transform everyday objects into works of art, nurturing a lifelong passion for creativity and innovation.

Reading

In the enchanting world of reading, students at Dr. Avinash Acharya Vidyalay’s primary section embark on exciting literary adventures, diving into a treasure trove of stories, poems, and informational texts.

Guided by passionate educators, they develop essential literacy skills, such as decoding, fluency, and comprehension, while exploring diverse genres and authors.

With each turn of the page, they expand their horizons, broaden their perspectives, and ignite their imaginations, discovering the transformative power of reading to educate, inspire, and empower them on their journey of lifelong learning.
